Some factors detected by facial skin analysis machine

Nowadays people pay more and more attention to the facial skin care. Facial skin analysis is becoming popular, helping people find skin problems and make improvements. It is also valuable for researchers

Wrinkles

Severity of facial wrinkles and fine lines.

Pores

Visible facial pores.

Visible Spots

Spots visible under the standard daylight conditions.

UV Invisible Spots

Hyperpigmented spots that are clearly visible under UV-fluorescence.

UV Porphyrins

Porphyrins that are visible as bright spots under UV-fluorescence.

RBX Red

Small red spots and vascular features based upon the RBX Red image.

RBX Brown

Hyperpigmented spots based upon the RBX Brown image.

COPROPORPHYRIN-III

COPROPORPHYRIN-III are visible as bright spots in a processed orange or green image.

Blue Invisible Spots

Hyperpigmented spots that are clearly visible under Blue light excitation imaging modality.

PROTOPORPHYRIN-IX

PROTOPORPHYRIN-IX are visible as bright spots in a processed orange or red image.

Different people have different facial skin features due to different gender, age, living area and daily habits. Aging, long-term sun exposure, improper diet, poor cleaning, and so on can cause above problems. To solve these problems, we need some methods and techniques to detect them.
