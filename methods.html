Methods related to facial aesthetic analysis

This page briefly introduces methods used in the field of facial aesthetic analysis -- age estimation.

Age Estimation

Age estimation is the determination of a person’s age based on biometric features. These biometric features are extracted from a person’s face. Age estimation results could help us understand the status of our skin in a scientific way and do skin care. The automated evaluation of skin age has very important research significance and application value for many industries.
